## KEY FEATURES (0:35 – 1:25)

**[SCREEN: Sections generating live, one after another, progress bar moving]**

> Generation happens section by section with full continuity — tone, context, and narrative flow maintained across the entire document.

**[SCREEN: Reviewer flags appearing. User clicks Fix on one paragraph. Diff overlay. Only that paragraph changes.]**

> The built-in Reviewer audits every paragraph. Fix issues surgically — one paragraph at a time. Everything else stays untouched.

**[SCREEN: AI Detection panel showing content scores — highlighting which sections read as AI-generated]**

> And here's what writers actually need: a **built-in AI detector**. LACON scores your content as you write — so you can refine sections that read too synthetic before you publish. No third-party tools. No guessing.

**[SCREEN: Research Workbench — PDFs uploaded, web sources pulled, fact-check panel active]**

> Research is built in. Upload PDFs, pull web sources, fact-check claims — all wired directly into your document.

**[SCREEN: Skills panel — Grant Writer, Academic, Storyteller personas. User stacks two.]**

> Writing Skills give your AI a specialized persona. Grant writer. Academic. Storyteller. Stack up to three.

**[SCREEN: Provider Settings — GPT, Claude, Gemini, Mistral in dropdown]**

> Model-agnostic. Bring GPT, Claude, Gemini, Mistral — your key, your choice. Your data never leaves your machine.
